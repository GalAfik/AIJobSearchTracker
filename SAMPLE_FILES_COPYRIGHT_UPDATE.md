## Rationale

### Why These Changes?

1. **Trademark Protection**
   - Real company names are registered trademarks
   - Using them in sample data could imply endorsement or affiliation
   - Avoids potential legal issues

2. **Copyright Compliance**
   - Sample data should not reference real companies
   - Modified names are clearly fictional
   - Maintains educational value without copyright concerns

3. **Professional Best Practice**
   - Industry standard for sample/demo data
   - Protects both developers and users
   - Prevents confusion with real job postings

### Company Name Strategy

**Pattern used:**
- **Minor spelling changes** (Amazon → Anazon, Apple → Appel)
- **Letter doubling** (Google → Gooogle, Adobe → Adobee)
- **Letter substitution** (Meta → Metta, Netflix → Netflux)
- **Generic descriptive names** (Salesforce → SalesForce Pro)

All modified names:
- ✅ Clearly not the real company
